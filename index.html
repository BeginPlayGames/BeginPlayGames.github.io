ABOUT THE GAME

Silent Life is a hardcore survival simulation set in an occupied territory. No monsters, no radiation - just the harsh reality of living under occupation. Every decision matters. Every resource counts. Will you survive?

KEY FEATURES

HARDCORE SURVIVAL

Manage hunger, thirst, and health in a world where resources are scarce and danger lurks around every corner.

STEALTH & TACTICS

Avoid patrols, hide in shadows, and make strategic decisions to stay alive in occupied territory.

RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Scavenge for supplies, craft tools, and make every item count in your struggle for survival.

MORAL CHOICES

Face difficult decisions that test your humanity. Will you help others or focus on your own survival?
